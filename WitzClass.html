The Process

From setup to graded
in four simple steps

01 — School
🏫
School Registers
The school admin registers, receives a unique school code, and shares it with their teachers to join the institution.
02 — Teacher
👨‍🏫
Teacher Creates Classroom
Teacher joins the school and creates a classroom. A WTZ join code is auto-generated and shared with students.
03 — Student
🎒
Students Enroll
Students enter the join code in seconds — no admin provisioning, no email invites. Just the code and they're in.
04 — Cycle
✅
Assign · Submit · Grade
Teacher posts assignments, students submit digitally, teacher grades with scores and feedback. Everyone notified in real time.

Our Story

Built from a real frustration.

A
Asiwaju
Founder & Chief Executive Officer

"I built WitzClass because I was tired of watching great teachers waste time chasing paper. Every minute spent managing homework is a minute stolen from actual teaching."

EdTech Founder Full-Stack Engineer Education Advocate Django & React

WitzClass was born from a simple, persistent problem — teachers drowning in paper, and students escaping accountability with three words: "I forgot it."

We started with a question: what if submitting an assignment was as easy as sharing a code? What if a teacher could see — at a glance — exactly who had submitted, who was late, and who hadn't bothered at all?

What if grading didn't mean shuffling stacks of paper or chasing students down corridors? What if the entire assignment lifecycle lived in one simple, reliable system?

That question became WitzClass. We started with the teacher's real problems and built backwards to the simplest possible solution — a platform that disappears into the background and lets education happen.

Platform Strategy

Two ways to use one
powerful platform

Deploy WitzClass as a complete system for your school, or embed our services directly into your existing LMS via SDK.

Standalone Platform
Complete System for Schools & Tutors
A fully self-contained assignment management system. Register, create classrooms, share join codes, and go — no additional setup required.
  • School registration with unique school codes
  • Teacher onboarding via school code
  • Classroom creation with WTZ join codes
  • Full assignment lifecycle — post, submit, grade
  • School admin dashboard with full visibility
  • Works for solo tutors and full institutions alike
Integration SDK — Coming Soon
Embed WitzClass Into Any LMS
External Learning Management Systems plug WitzClass directly into their platform via our SDK — without rebuilding assignment infrastructure from scratch.
  • RESTful assignment creation & distribution APIs
  • Embeddable submission collection UI components
  • Grading & tracking as a managed service
  • White-label compatible
  • Swagger/OpenAPI documentation
  • Webhooks for real-time event integration
